WHO WE ARE

The Driving Force Behind Sri Lanka's Modern Industry

OUR STORY

Pamankada Group was established with a singular vision: to empower the Sri Lankan workforce with professional-grade tools at accessible prices. Recognizing a gap in the market for high-quality, durable, and affordable machinery, we partnered with global giants.

Today, we stand proud as the authorized distributor for INGCO, WADFOW, and DECAKILA. From the construction site to the modern kitchen, we power daily life with reliability and innovation.

OUR VISION

To be the premier provider of industrial and home solutions in Sri Lanka, recognized for quality, innovation, and unwavering customer trust.

OUR MISSION

To deliver world-class tools and appliances that enhance productivity and quality of life, backed by exceptional service and competitive pricing.

OUR VALUES

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Customer-Centricity define every decision we make and every product we sell.
